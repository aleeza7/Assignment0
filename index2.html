Born To Die (2012)

A relationship you want to have and continue but you just can’t keep going because you are heading different directions in life. Themes of love, sacrifice, freedom, Americana and feeling content (in some songs) as well as wanting better (in others.)

Paradise Edition: The fall of man, freedom, the open road. Themes of freedom, an idyllic world and religion.

Ultraviolence (2014)

Fragility, loose feeling/ jazz feeling. A possibly sardonic and partially ironic introspective. Themes of pain, being misunderstood, Cocaine, and the West Coast.

Honeymoon (2015)

Solitude and hoping everything will turn out beautifully for you. Themes of Romance, loneliness, trying and desperation.

Lust For Life (2017)

Not Happy but positive. The first album that really looks to today and not the past. A commentary on the political climate of today as well as a personal shift from what she’s done before. Themes of 70s hippie culture, impending doom as well as finding a place for yourself and love & freedom.

Norman F******* Rockwell (2019)

Being Misunderstood. Rock and roll is dead and the world is on fire but the record sounds like a nostalgic Americana ocean. Themes of being misunderstood, hope and finding happiness.

Chemtrails Over The Country Club (2021)

Family and relationships. Finding people who make you feel understood and being your own person. Themes of self acceptance, looking forward, being sure of what you want and going for it and being wild and free.

Blue banisters (2021)

Having people who support you. Loving yourself and who you’ve become. Having a good close group of people to love and be loved by. Themes of self love, friendship, family, being wild, not relying on a man to take care of you.
